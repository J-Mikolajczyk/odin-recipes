Ranch Pretzels

A kid-friendly pigs in a blanket recipe made with refrigerated biscuit dough and hot dogs.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz biscuit dough
  • 8 slices American cheese
  • 8 authentic frankfurters, but any hot dog will do

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Shape biscuit dough sections into 3-inch diameter circles.
  3. Lay a slice of cheese on each circle, place a frankfurter on each slice, and roll it up.
  4. Place rolls, seam-side down, on a cookie sheet and bake until golden brown. About 10 to 15 minutes
