Chicken Wing Dip

A spicy chicken wing dip that's great for parties, family game nights, and even tailgates

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs sharp cheddar (shredded or block)
  • 16 oz cream cheese
  • 24 oz chicken breast (or four cans)
  • Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  • Ranch

Steps

  1. IF USING FRESH CHICKEN: Cook by any method and shred with forks or electric mixer
  2. IF USING BLOCK CHEESE: Shred cheese by any preferred method
  3. Put all cheddar and cream cheese into a crock pot set to high
  4. After cream cheese is softened and most cheddar is melted, mix in chicken
  5. Begin to add ranch and hot sauce to taste
  6. The dish is completed when the dip reaches a high temperature
